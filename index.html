About Carbon

Name: carbon computers

Website: www.carbon.co

Industry: Consumer Technology, Personal Computing

Company Personality: Technical + Friendly Layperson

Customer Segment:

  • Primary - People who enjoy games but lack the knowledge of component selection, building a computer, or hardware performance
  • Secondary - People buying for others; don't know/care for the details, just roughly know what kind of games the computer is meant for
  • Tertiary - Enthusiasts that have the indepth technical knowledge, but lack the time or care to build a system

Value Proposition

  • Providing systems not based solely around specs, but offer/suggest its capabilities in experiences; games, workloads, etc.
